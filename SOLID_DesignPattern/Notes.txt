Motivation for Single Responsibility Principle:
   Maintainability
   Testability
   Flexibility and Extensibility
   Parallel Development
   Loose Coulping
   SOLID Principles and design Patterns play key role in the above motiivations

Open Closed Principle:
    any new functionality should be implemented by adding new classes, attributes and methods,
     instead of changing the current oen ot existing ones. 

Liskov Substitution Principle:
    S is a substype of T, then objects of type T may be replaced with objects of type S.
